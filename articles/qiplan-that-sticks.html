A QI plan that sticks: from good intentions to measurable change

What makes a QI plan actionable: ownership, small tests of change, measurement that matters, and honest reporting.

Key takeaways

  • Keep aims specific and patient-centred; avoid vague ambitions that can’t be measured.
  • Assign ownership and timelines; improvement without owners becomes background noise.
  • Test small, learn fast: PDSA cycles help teams adapt safely and quickly.
  • Report progress honestly: what changed, what didn’t, and what’s next.
